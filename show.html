Number of days open

This graph shows how long issues are open, and how they move between different columns on the project board. Some things to look out for:

  • Dark areas show where many issues spend a lot of time. If you have one column label that's much darker than the others, it could indicate a bottleneck in your workflow.
  • Issues that have spent a very long time in one column are suspcious. They might be blocked, forgotten, or duplicates.
